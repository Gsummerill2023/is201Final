About Your Stylist - ME!

I love the beach and getting lost in the sand under the warm sand. In 2022 I cut hair, my now Fiance's, for the first time. Initially shocked at what I had done I found that I fell in love with it! My family consists of many cosmetologists and barber's and in a way I have family trait. I fell in love with cosmetology and in 2023 I began school at MTech

Graduating in January 2024, I have developed my skills in talents around Hair coloring, Cuttting and styling, Hair Extensions, and Eyelashes. Not only have I become a professional cosmetologist, I am also a certified Eyelash Technician. Wanting to bring beauty and the beach to everyday life, I created my company Sunkissed Hair so that you can feel like your best self!

I guarantee that you will fall in love with your new hair. Join us today to start your best, care free, beautiful hair life!
